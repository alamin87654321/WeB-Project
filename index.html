About Fresh Harvest

Our Story

Fresh Harvest was founded in 2022 with a simple mission to make fresh, high-quality groceries accessible to everyone. We started as a small local delivery service and have grown into one of the leading online grocery platforms in Bangladesh.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing our customers with the freshest products at competitive prices while supporting local farmers and producers. We believe in sustainable sourcing and reducing food waste through efficient supply chain management.

Why Choose Us?

  • Fresh products sourced directly from farmers
  • Competitive prices with regular discounts
  • Fast and reliable delivery service
  • Easy-to-use online platform
  • Secure payment options
  • Excellent customer support
